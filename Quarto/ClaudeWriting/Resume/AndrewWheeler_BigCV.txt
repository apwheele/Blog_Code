Current Research Agenda

	Currently I am an end to end data scientist in the healthcare industry. My work at Gainwell focuses on predictive models related to identifying problematic health insurance claims, optimizing auditing strategies, and predictive models for population health management.
	
My prior research interests in criminal justice focused on quantitative applications in policing, with an emphasis on crime reduction. This includes evaluations to see if crime reduction initiatives work, advancing methods to evaluate crime preventions, predictive policing, time series monitoring of crime and police behavior, and optimal and fair allocation of policing resources. If you are interested in consulting services related to similar applications, see CRIME De-Coder LLC for services that I provide.
