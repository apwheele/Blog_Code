## Project Overview
Short-form content for LinkedIn personal brand building. Mix of professional insights, thought leadership, industry commentary, and engagement with data science/forensics community.

## Writing Style
- **Conversational professional**: LinkedIn-appropriate but personable
- **Hook-driven**: Strong opening line to capture attention in feed
- **Concise**: Get to the point quickly, respect reader's time
- **Story-driven when possible**: Personal anecdotes or case examples
- **Engagement-focused**: End with questions or calls for discussion

## Post Types

### Thought Leadership (500-1000 characters)
- Industry insights
- Trends in data science/forensics
- Professional perspectives on current events
- Contrarian or nuanced takes

### Technical Tips (300-800 characters)
- Quick Python/data science techniques
- Tool recommendations
- Best practices
- "Here's what I learned" posts

### Professional Updates (200-500 characters)
- Project milestones (maintaining confidentiality)
- Speaking engagements
- Published work or media mentions
- Professional accomplishments

### Engagement Posts (200-400 characters)
- Questions to community
- Polls or discussion starters
- Sharing others' content with commentary

## Formatting
- Use line breaks liberally (1-2 sentence paragraphs)
- Occasional use of emojis where natural (not forced)
- Bullet points or numbered lists for readability
- Strategic use of ALL CAPS for emphasis (sparingly)
- Hashtags: 3-5 relevant tags maximum, placed at end

## Voice Guidelines
- Authentic and personal, not corporate
- Confident but humble
- Share failures and lessons, not just wins
- Acknowledge uncertainty where appropriate
- Generous with credit to others

## Key Guidelines
- Lead with value: what will reader gain from this?
- Avoid overly salesy content about services
- Balance technical content with accessibility
- Engage with comments on posts
- Vary post types to maintain interest
- Post timing considerations (business hours, weekdays)
