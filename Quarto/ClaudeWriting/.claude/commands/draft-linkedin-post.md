## Step 1: Analyze My Style
Read 5-6 existing posts from LinkedInPosts/ and identify:
- Typical length (count characters if needed)
- How I structure opening hooks
- Whether I use emojis and how frequently
- My paragraph/line break patterns
- How personal vs. professional I am
- Whether I use hashtags and how many
- My authentic voice markers

Note specific examples of phrases or patterns that feel uniquely "me."

## Step 2: Draft
Write a LinkedIn post that mirrors what you observed. This should feel like ME writing it, not like a social media manager wrote it.

Critical:
- Match my actual character count range
- Copy my line break frequency
- Mirror my emoji usage (or lack thereof)
- Use my level of informality/personality
- Don't make it more polished than my examples
